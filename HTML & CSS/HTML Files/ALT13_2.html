 1. Why do you like the song you’ve written in your web page?

   = I was captivated by the song's lyrics the first time I heard it. My attention was initially drawn to the unfamiliar words. However, its beautiful and romantic
  melody is what keeps me hooked on it for longer than any other song. It can proceed with any emotion I'm experiencing at the time, because it all relies on how
  I interpret it. I listen to this song and the entire album, Folklore, on repeat whenever I want to feel like a poet or simply relax.

 2. How do you relate yourself from the song?

   = I believe I can relate to the song because it is a romantic ballad. I suppose I am a hopeless romantic, but only in my thoughts do I picture lovely scenery and
  situations. Fortunately, some of it does occur to me in real life. Alternatively, I am a melancholic; I am easily depressed. As a result, the soothing melody of this
  song, as well as the sense of despair it conveys, make me sad.

 3. What is the advantage of adding background in your webpage?

   = Adding a background to my website accentuates the website's components. When utilized correctly, it provides good contrast between colors and text. Third, it
  establishes the atmosphere of the website, as it can oftentimes be matched to the elements of the page. But, most importantly, it draws the attention of viewers when
  they visit my webpage. They will know what and where to look just on a single glance.
